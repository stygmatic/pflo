About Me

I am a 13 year old middle schooler in Massachusetts. I mainly like electronics, engineering, coding, 3D printing, and cats. I have been doing electronics and engineering since I was 5, when I got an Arduino kit. I also participate in Hack Club, where I received a $100 grant to work on one of my projects—a massive tank—and another $100 grant to order custom PCBs that I design.

I sometimes post videos on my YouTube channel, Ewoud Van Vooren. My most watched long-form video has 1.7k views, and I have about 130 subscribers.

I have 2 cats, Luna (2 years old) and Cosmo (1 year old). I was in a group that launched a weather balloon with one of my projects on it, a project to analyze current tree coverage and compare it to past coverage.

I am also on the leadership team for my local makerspace, where I conduct multiple classes—including Latin.
